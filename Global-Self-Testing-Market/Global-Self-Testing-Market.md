<h5><strong><u>Market Restraining Factors</u></strong></h5>
<ul>
<li>
<h4><strong>Ensuring Reliable Results in the Japan Self-Testing Market</strong></h4>
</li>
</ul>
<p>One of the primary challenges in such a Japan Self-Testing Market is the accuracy in the result produced by these devices. Although the self-testing solutions may be convenient, they are to be much more precise in results so that there is no risk of misdiagnosis or late treatments. Such a false result may prompt patients to do wrong health decisions, such as delaying prescribed medical interventions or even initiating unnecessary treatment. This brings forth possible health consequences as well as consumer distrust over the use of self-testing kits. For example, a false-negative pregnancy test reading or a reading of low blood sugar may result in improper health management or failure to visit a health care provider for further evaluation. Thus, manufacturers have to continuously improve the testing technology, ensure adherence to the regulatory standard, and emphasize education and training of the users to make self-testing products more reliable and valid. Only then can these devices gain widespread adoptions and sustain consumer confidence.</p>
